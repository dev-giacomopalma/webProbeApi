One of the nicest solid colognes I've tried by a mile.

Julio & Jack's Explorer has been by go to solid colgne for a while. But they seem to have gone under as no response from them and no stock anywhere, so I've been looking for an alternative.

Aromantom's Ocean sounded very similar from the description so I thoight I'd give it a try... and damn I'm pleased I did! This actually improves on the Explorer, a refreshing light almost alpiney kind of scent, but this has a little more warmth to it aswell.

I really dont know how to describe how it smells, other than amazing!

The container is perfect and fits in your mini jeans jonny pocket so you can always have it on you, and one or two light rubs on the neck will last you all day.

Having tried 4 ot er conpanies of solid cologne (including the mega expensive Fulton & Roark from the US) to me this is by FAR the best scent I've tried.

Top that off with the lowest price of any solid cologne and the fact it's a UK brand and its win win all round.

Highly highly recomended, and can't wait to try the rest of the range.
